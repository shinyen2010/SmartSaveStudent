# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Product Name

SmartSave Student

## Tagline

"Spend Wisely. Save Smarter."

---

# Executive Summary

SmartSave Student is an AI-powered personal finance platform designed specifically for students. The app helps students track expenses, manage budgets, achieve savings goals, improve financial literacy, and develop healthy spending habits through intelligent insights, gamification, and personalized recommendations.

The platform addresses a common student challenge: managing limited financial resources while balancing education, lifestyle, and future financial goals.

---

# Problem Statement

Students often struggle with:

* Poor budgeting habits
* Impulse spending
* Lack of financial literacy
* Difficulty tracking expenses
* Inconsistent savings behavior
* Financial stress before month-end
* Lack of awareness about spending patterns

As a result, many students fail to save money and develop long-term financial discipline.

---

# Product Vision

To become the leading financial wellness platform for students by combining budgeting, savings, AI-powered coaching, financial education, and gamified habit building into one seamless experience.

---

## Business Goals
